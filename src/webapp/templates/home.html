INSIGHTS
Helpful data
BlazeBlock offers real-time information about fire incidents and areas at higher risk, helping users make informed decisions about their safety and evacuation plans. Users gain insights into the state of local forests, nearby reservations or even their neighbourhood. They can monitor trends and take appropriate action.
REPORTS
Help your community
By enabling civilians to report and monitor fire incidents and access real-time data on potential risks, it empowers individuals and communities to actively engage in fire prevention and safety efforts. Simultaneously, it provides authorized authorities with tools to monitor and manage fire incidents efficiently, all while fostering collaboration with local communities.
DATA AGGREGATION
All useful data in one single place
Our app serves as a robust platform for data aggregation sensor data, satellite imagery, and GIS mapping with user-generated data to provide information about spreading wildfires. BlazeBlock serves as a centralized repository of data, increasing effectiveness and saving time in critical situations.
